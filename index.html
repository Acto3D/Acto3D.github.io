Advanced Visualization Tools

Real-Time Rendering

Experience fluid, interactive exploration of volumetric microscopy data with rendering optimized for Apple Silicon's powerful GPU capabilities.

Transfer Functions

Streamlined Workflow

From image acquisition to stunning visualization in five comprehensive steps

01

Acquire Microscopy Images

Capture your fluorescence microscopy images with your preferred imaging system. Acto3D supports 3D reconstruction from a wide range of fluorescence microscopy techniques, including light sheet, confocal and multiphoton microscopy.

02

Convert to TIFF with Fiji

Use Fiji (ImageJ) to process and convert your microscopy images to TIFF format, preparing them for 3D reconstruction and visualization.

03

Import Into Acto3D

Simply load your processed TIFF files into Acto3D to visualize them in 3D space. Instantly explore your data from multiple viewing angles for detailed inspection.

04

Fine-Tune Visualization

Use Acto3D's intuitive transfer function editor to perfect color and opacity across multiple channels, enhancing contrast and highlighting specific features within your complex volumetric data.

05

Create Dynamic Presentations

Acto3D enables immersive and interactive 3D visualizations that can be used across a variety of contexts—including presentations, publications, and education. Built-in animation tools further enhance your ability to communicate complex structures with compelling visual impact.

Unlike commercial software, Acto3D is fully open-source, letting you customize every aspect of the GPU rendering process. Advanced users gain full insight into how their microscopy data is visualized.

The key advantage of volume rendering is the ability to adjust visualization using transfer functions. Acto3D provides programmatic control over these functions for more detailed and precise tuning.
