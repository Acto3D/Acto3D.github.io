Acto3D was developed with the aim of enabling researchers to easily observe multi-channel Z stack images in 3D captured with a fluorescent microscope. Opacity and color tone can be freely adjusted, and it fully supports any observation direction, any section, and MRP display.
For expert users, there's also an option to directly edit the transfer function.
